Our Story

We’re on a mission to completely revolutionize the laundry industry by putting the planet and your health first.

How It Started

Redefining Garment Care.

Founded in 2020, EcoWash began with a simple realization: standard dry cleaning methods were relying heavily on PERC, a toxic petrochemical that harms both human health and the environment.

We knew there had to be a better way. After months of research, we adopted advanced wet-cleaning technology and liquid silicone dry cleaning—methods that perfectly preserve fabrics while leaving a zero toxic footprint. Today, we process thousands of garments a week, all picked up and delivered in 100% electric vehicles.

Our Core Values

Everything we do at EcoWash revolves around preserving your clothes and protecting our planet.

Sustainability First

We use biodegradable solvents and strictly ban toxic chemicals to ensure clean water systems.

Unmatched Quality

Eco-friendly doesn't mean compromising. Our methods are gentler, leaving clothes softer and brighter.

Zero Plastic

We package all orders in reusable garment bags and collect hangers to drastically cut single-use plastic.

Community Focus

We proudly pay our staff living wages and actively donate clothing to local shelters every quarter.
