The Fine Print (But Greener)

Free Electric Delivery

Delivery is absolutely free on all orders over $30. For smaller orders, a flat $5 eco-delivery fee applies to cover our electric fleet costs.

Our standard zero-emission wash process takes 48 hours from pickup to drop-off. Need it faster? Opt for our 24-hour rush service for a 25% surcharge.

On your first order, you will receive a complimentary EcoWash reusable garment bag. Please return your hangers with each pickup so we can recycle them!
